GIS the way it was meant to be

Deployment

NapkinGIS can be molded to suit any workload.

The Napkin team has built a fully modular, web-based GIS software that can be deployed and installed under any system. Not only can the customer molded NapkinGIS to their liking by picking and choosing only the functionalities you need, the custom can even run NapkinGIS under their own infrastructure. This gives the customer full control over what data the NapkinGIS software package has access to and how it handes that data, as well as never paying for functionalities that will never be used. This also gives the customer absolute control over all configurations of the software, and with the ability to set any preference you may find suitable.

Integration

NapkinGIS being deployable, makes it fully integrable.

Thanks to the fact that NapkinGIS can be deployed under local systems, we are able to offer full integration of NapkinGIS with all your existing systems. The result of this is a software where your entire workflow can be incorporated into NapkinGIS, giving you an unprecedented overview and insight into the entire operation. This integration makes us able to guarantee a level of security and data-integrity that other GIS solutions can only promise. This makes NapkinGIS perfectly suited to data-sensitive or security-sensitive workloads.

Custom features

Making NapkinGIS do the job the way you want it.

Thanks to NapkinGIS being built fully modular, you are able to create and install custom-built features and plug-ins through our development library that satisfy your workloads particular needs. No job or workload is out of reach of NapkinGIS, and with the right features NapkinGIS can be molded to fit any job in the tool-chain.
